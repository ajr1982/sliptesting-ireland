Slip Testing for Ireland

Construction Testing Services Ltd, based in Antrim Northern Ireland have full UKAS accreditation for the comprehensive measurement of Slip Resistance of Surfaces using the ‘Pendulum' Tester (HSE’s preferred method of ‘slipperiness assessment.’)

We are available for Slip Testing throughout all of Ireland, including Dublin, Belfast, Galway and Londonderry.
